iPhone 16 Pro Max

The iPhone 16 Pro Max is Apple's latest, largest and has the longest-lasting battery of any iPhone Apple has released, It supports Apple's all-new "Apple Intelligence", created to assist you with writing, can generate images using AI, and will draw from your personal context to help with Siri. The iPhone 16 Pro Max measures 163 millimeters long, 77.6 millimeters wide, and 8.25 millimeters thin, making it larger than Apple's previously largest phone, the iPhone 15 Pro Max. Not only all this but it also allows you to type using braille without the need for a physical braille keyboard, making it accessible to those with visual impairments.

Samsung Galaxy S24 FE

The Samsung Galaxy S24 FE is one of the newest phones released by Samsung. It measures 162 millimeters long, 77.30 millimeters wide, and 8 millimeters thin, making it just a bit smaller than the iPhone 16 Pro Max above. On top of this, the phone has 24 hours battery to last you the entire day without running out of charge, three rear cameras all with amazing camera quality, and comes in a variety of colors to match just the way you want it to look. The Galaxy S24 FE is also waterproof and made to be tougher than previous Samsung phones that may have broken more easily.

Google Pixel 9 Pro

The Google Pixel 9 Pro is one of the first flagship AI phones, meaning that almost everything you do on this phone will have an AI integrated and ready to assist you with your current task, need to find something on Google? There's an AI to help. You've taken a photo of a meal and have forgotten what it's called. There's an AI that will tell you exactly what it is. The Google Pixel 9 Pro is built to be small, being 152.8 millimeters long, 72 millimeters wide, and 8.5 millimeters thin, while it may be too small for some people, it's perfect for those who prefer the smaller screen. It has 3 rear Cameras and amazing camera quality on top of that.
